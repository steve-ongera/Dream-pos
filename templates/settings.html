Frequently Asked Questions

How do I process a return?
Go to Sales History, find the transaction, and select "Process Return". Choose items to return and specify the reason.
Can I customize receipt templates?
Yes, navigate to Settings > Receipt Settings to customize your business information and receipt layout.
How do I add new products?
Go to Inventory > Add Product. Enter product details and pricing information.
